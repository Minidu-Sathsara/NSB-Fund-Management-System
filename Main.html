History of Our Company

NSB Fund Management, established in 1990, began as a small
boutique investment firm focused on providing personalized
financial services.

Over the years, it has grown into a leading fund management company, renowned for its expertise in portfolio
management, innovative investment strategies, and commitment to client satisfaction. With a strong foundation
built on trust and performance, NSB Fund Management continues to expand its offerings, adapting to market
changes and leveraging opportunities for growth and stability in the financial sector.
